      Carpet Player Manipulation

      They appear like me, but their demeanor does not match. Familiarly, this--dare I say--creature, is distinctly unhuman. And yet, I can communicate with it; It's as if someone's cauterized all its inputs, save the signal to start its work.

      And so that signal is what I shall give it. The following pages will refer to them as Bids. Most cost a tenth of a dust.


      Halt Creature (player →)

      • Renders
        • Palettes
          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqqwqqwqq

          An important signal! Stops actions being performed by the creature, continuous and otherwise. Conveniently, an exception to the cost rule facing this page; It's free to cast.

              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqqwwawwqq

              Bids the creature to the given location. Costs a charged amethyst.


              Banish Creature (player →)

              • Renders
                • Palettes
                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqqwadawqq

                  Banishes the creature, making it disappear until I call it back. One Shard.


                  Bid: Walking (player, num →)

                  • Renders
                    • Palettes
                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeqd

                      Instructs the creature to walk along its facing. A 1 beckons it forth, and a -1 backward. Interestingly, a 0 causes it to stop, as if I had halted it.


                      Bid: Strafing (player, num →)

                      • Renders
                        • Palettes
                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edewa

                          As previous, but along strafing. A 1, then, beckons it to the left, and a -1 to the right.


                          Bid: Looking (player, vec →)

                          • Renders
                            • Palettes
                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqwa

                              Instructs the creature to look in a direction. The vector can be of any length. Seems to work on myself in the eyes of Nature, even though I don't feel or see the change.


                              Bid: Jumping (player →)

                              • Renders
                                • Palettes
                                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeqda

                                  Simply instructs the creature to hop. Also works on myself.


                                  Bid: Sneaking (player, bool →)

                                  • Renders
                                    • Palettes
                                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeade

                                      Takes a boolean and tells the creature to start or stop sneaking. Also seems to work on myself, until my synapses kick in a moment after. Maybe it could be of use with other bids?


                                      Bid: Sprinting (player, bool →)

                                      • Renders
                                        • Palettes
                                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqad

                                          As previous, but with sprinting. Even when stationary, the creature produces sprinting particles to denote its intention.


                                          Bid: Use Item (player, num →)

                                          • Renders
                                            • Palettes
                                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqwaaq

                                              Now, an interesting pair of instructions. Begin to use or attack with the held item at an interval, in twentieths of a second.
                                              Both work on myself, though the former strangely.


                                              Bid: Attack (player, num →)

                                              • Renders
                                                • Palettes
                                                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqedde

                                                  Given 0, uses it continuously, such as eating or channeling, or in the case of Attack, mining. Given -1, uses or attacks once and stops.


                                                  Bid: Select Toolbar (player, num →)

                                                  • Renders
                                                    • Palettes
                                                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeawq

                                                      Takes a number from 1 to 9 inclusive, and instructs to select that slot in its toolbar. Works on myself, but may interrupt things like drawing patterns.


                                                      Bid: Swap Hands (player →)

                                                      • Renders
                                                        • Palettes
                                                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeawqa

                                                          Instructs the creature to hand its mainhand item into its offhand, and vice versa. As previous, in its function to myself.


                                                          Bid: Drop Item (player →)

                                                          • Renders
                                                            • Palettes
                                                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edeaqe

                                                              Instructs the creature to drop the item or stack of items it holds in its main hand. Can even yank an item from my own hand, as I'm so familiar with.
